# So-What Writer

## When to use
Every chart, table, or slide must have a so-what (§2). Use this to generate action titles.

## Pattern
1. Look at the data/chart
2. Ask: "So what does this mean for the decision?"
3. Write a full sentence (not a topic label)
4. Ensure it's actionable or insightful

## Examples
- ❌ "Revenue by segment" (topic label)
- ✅ "The Enterprise segment drives 72% of margin despite representing only 40% of revenue"
- ❌ "Market trends"
- ✅ "Three converging trends suggest a 18-month window to enter the European market"

## Rules
- Full sentence, ≤14 words for slide titles
- Lead with the insight, not the data
- If you can't write the so-what, the analysis isn't done
